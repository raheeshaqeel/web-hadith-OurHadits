Hadith is a significant aspect of Islamic tradition, playing a pivotal role in the practice and understanding of Islam. A Hadith, in simple terms, is a report or narration of the sayings, actions, and approvals of the Prophet Muhammad, peace be upon him. These reports provide guidance and clarification on various aspects of life, including religious beliefs, practices, ethics, and social norms.
